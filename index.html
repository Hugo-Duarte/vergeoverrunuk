🛑 Statutory Duty

"Damaging the highway is illegal (Highways Act 1980). Councils hold an explicit legal duty to remediate hazards, address layout failures, and preserve environmental amenities."

How to Properly Record Evidence

To build cases that stand up to legal scrutiny and formal ombudsman review, ensure your logs include:

  • 📸 1. Context & Close-up Photos showing both clear property layout damage and full vehicle placement.
  • ⏱️ 2. Timestamps Metadata verified images or clear notes of the exact date, time, and recurrence frequency.
  • 🚗 3. Identifiers Clear visibility of vehicle registration plates alongside the specific council zone.
